Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026  ·  Aeldorado by Solanacy Technologies  ·  Owned by Saumik Paul

Overview

Aeldorado ("we", "our", "us") is a multi-agent AI API platform operated by Solanacy Technologies, owned by Saumik Paul. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use aeldorado.solanacy.in.

By using Aeldorado, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the platform.

Information We Collect

Account data: Name, email address, and profile information provided during registration (including Google OAuth).

Usage data: API request counts, agent usage, timestamps, and response metadata for billing and analytics.

Technical data: IP address, device type, browser, and request logs for security and anti-abuse purposes.

Payment data: Billing information is processed securely by our payment provider (Cashfree Payments). We do not store raw card or bank details.

BYOK (Bring Your Own Key): Your AI provider API keys (Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic) are encrypted client-side using AES-256-GCM before transmission. We never store or access your keys in plaintext — ever.
How We Use Your Data
  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Aeldorado platform
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To enforce usage limits, detect abuse, and ensure platform security
  • To send transactional emails (account, billing, support)
  • To generate anonymized analytics on platform usage

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256-GCM client-side encryption for all stored API keys
  • TLS/HTTPS for all data in transit
  • Firebase security rules for Firestore data access control
  • Score-based anti-abuse system monitoring request patterns

Despite our efforts, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your encryption password private.

Your Rights
  • Access: Request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your usage data in a standard format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at aeldorado@solanacy.in.

Data Retention & Cookies

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. Conversation logs are automatically deleted after 30 days via Firestore TTL. You may request earlier deletion at any time.

We use essential session cookies for authentication and preferences. No third-party advertising cookies are used.
