Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026  ·  Aeldorado by Solanacy Technologies  ·  Owned by Saumik Paul

Overview

We want every developer to have a great experience on Aeldorado. This policy outlines when refunds are available, how to cancel your subscription, and what to expect from the process.

This policy applies to all paid plans — Starter, Growth, Pro, and Enterprise — purchased on aeldorado.solanacy.in.

Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a refund in the following cases:

Eligible
  • Technical failure: Platform is inaccessible for more than 48 consecutive hours due to our fault
  • Duplicate charge: You were charged more than once for the same billing period
  • Unauthorized transaction: A charge occurred without your knowledge or consent
  • First-time upgrade: Refund within 7 days of your first paid plan upgrade if the platform does not meet the described capabilities

The following are not eligible for refund:

Not eligible
  • Partial use of a billing period after cancellation
  • Exceeding request limits on the Free plan
  • Downgrade from a higher plan mid-cycle
  • Account suspension due to Terms of Service violations
  • Charges from your AI provider (Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic) — those are separate contracts
How to Request a Refund
1
Email us
Send a refund request to aeldorado@solanacy.in with subject line: "Refund Request — [Your Email]"
2
Include your details
Provide your registered email, account ID, transaction ID, amount, and reason for the refund.
3
Review period
We review all requests within 3–5 business days and respond with a decision.
4
Refund processed
Approved refunds are credited to the original payment method within 7–10 business days, subject to your bank's processing time.
Cancellation
  • You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings dashboard
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle — you retain access until then
  • Cancelling does not trigger an automatic refund for the current period
  • Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation before being deleted
Downgrade to Free anytime — no penalty
Contact Us

For refund requests, billing questions, or subscription issues:

Email us and we'll get back to you within 24–48 business hours.
